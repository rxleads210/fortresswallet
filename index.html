Why FortressWallet

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built around one principle: you control your crypto, not us, not anyone else.

Offline Key Storage

Private keys are generated and stored exclusively on the Secure Element chip. They never connect to any network, ever.

Physical Confirmation

Every transaction must be manually confirmed by pressing the physical buttons on the device. No remote approval possible.

Secure Element Chip

CC EAL-certified chip — the same technology used in passports and banking cards. Resistant to physical and side-channel attacks.

USB-C & Bluetooth 5.0

Connect to your phone wirelessly or to your desktop via USB-C. Full cross-platform companion app for iOS, Android, Windows & macOS.

24-Word Seed Recovery

Standard BIP-39 seed phrase. If your device is lost or broken, restore everything on any compatible wallet — we're never in the loop.

PIN Lock & Auto-Wipe

Set your own PIN. Too many wrong entries triggers a full factory wipe — your keys cannot be brute-forced by anyone who finds your device.

Zero Trust Design

Designed so we
can't touch your keys.

Even if we wanted to access your funds — we can't. The architecture makes it structurally impossible.

Ships completely blank

The device arrives with zero data. No keys, no pre-loaded seed, nothing. Factory sealed — you can verify at first boot.

Seed generated on-chip, never transmitted

Your 24-word seed phrase is generated entirely on the device during first setup. It is mathematically inaccessible from outside the chip.

Signing happens inside the chip

Transaction data enters the Secure Element, the signature exits — the private key never leaves. Not even partially.
